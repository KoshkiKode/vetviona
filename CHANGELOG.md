## [1.0.0] — 2026-04-12

### Added
- **Interactive tree builder** — add people, link parents/children and spouses, pan & zoom tree diagram.
- **Source citations** — attach evidence to birth, marriage and death facts; manage sources in a dedicated screen.
- **Historical place matching** — date-aware autocomplete covering Europe, Asia, Africa, Americas and Oceania with colonial and indigenous name layers.
- **RootLoop™ Sync** — zero-knowledge encrypted device-to-device sync over WiFi (Auto) and Bluetooth/local (Manual).
- **GEDCOM import & export** (Desktop Pro) — industry-standard format; resumable import with deduplication merge mode.
- **Backup & Restore** — full tree export to JSON; one-tap restore.
- **PDF family book** — narrative-style PDF export of the whole tree.
- **CSV export** — tabular export for spreadsheet use.
- **Life timelines** — chronological view of each person's key events.
- **Relationship Finder** — instantly see how two people in the tree are related.
- **Relationship certificate** — printable certificate for any relationship path.
- **Descendants chart** — top-down visual chart of all descendants from any person.
- **Pedigree view** — fan-chart ancestor view.
- **Family timeline** — overlapping lifespans across generations.
- **Medical history** — record and view conditions, treatments, and family health patterns.
- **Research tasks** — built-in to-do list for genealogy research actions.
- **Calendar** — upcoming birthdays and wedding anniversaries.
- **Statistics** — person count, average lifespan, surname frequency and more.
- **Custom themes** — pick any primary colour; dark mode supported.
- **Multiple family trees** (Mobile Paid / Desktop Pro) — create, rename and switch between separate trees.
- **In-app purchase** (Android/iOS) — one-time Mobile Paid upgrade with on-device receipt persistence and restore.
- **Onboarding flow** — three-screen introduction shown on first launch.
- **Encrypted local PIN** — optional username/password lock to protect tree data on the device.
- **Privacy Policy & Terms of Service** links in Settings.

### Platforms

- iOS (IPA, free and paid tiers)
