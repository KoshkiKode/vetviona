RootLoop™

RootLoop™ is Vetviona's proprietary sync layer that lets your devices exchange encrypted family-tree data directly — without relying on any external server. It comes in two tiers:

RootLoop Auto

Full WiFi sync that runs automatically whenever your devices join the same home network — like a client-server pair that just works.

Mobile Paid & Desktop Pro
RootLoop Manual

On-demand sync you trigger inside the app when you're right in front of your computer — over Bluetooth or any local connection you initiate.

All tiers (including Mobile Free)

Desktop Pro supports both free and paid mobile devices. When a free-tier mobile is connected, sync falls back to manual-only and is capped at 100 people per session.
