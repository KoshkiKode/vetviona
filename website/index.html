What Ancestrova does

Ancestrova is a modern family history app for building trees, attaching sources, importing records, and syncing devices directly with encrypted local-first technology.

Native place matching GEDCOM support Source pages Tree diagrams WiFi sync Bluetooth sync

RootLoop™

RootLoop™ is the proprietary sync layer that lets your devices exchange encrypted family-tree data locally and keep everything aligned without relying on a server you maintain.

Version: 1.0.0

RootLoop™ can evolve independently from the app itself.

Features

Tree building

Add people, link relationships, set marriages, and keep records organized.

Sources

Attach citations to specific facts and keep person records clean.

Places

Historical and modern place matching with date-aware autocomplete.

Sync

Encrypted local sync across devices using RootLoop™.

Export

Standard GEDCOM export for portability.

Themes

Custom colors, synced across your devices when enabled.
